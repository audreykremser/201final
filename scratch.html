In 2022, Taylor Swift announced the Eras Tour, a worldwide stadium tour consisting of songs from all throughout her career. Since Taylor Swift's last tour, the Reputation Stadium Tour, Taylor Swift had released 4 new albums that she wanted to perform. Instead of picking an album for the theme of the tour, she decided to perform songs from all different eras of her musical career. Taylor Swift began performing this tour at the beginning of 2023 and has performance dates scheduled through to the end of 2024. Here is an image of Taylor Swift performing the "Lover" set of the Eras Tour:

Taylor Swift is planning to rerelease her first 6 albums as "Taylor's Version" in order to own the rights to the music. In order to gain some popularity for the rerecorded albums, Taylor Swift has added tracks listed as "from the vault" to each album. These are previously unreleased songs that Taylor Swift is putting out on the rerecords. She has already released 4 out of the 6 albums that she intends to rerecord.
